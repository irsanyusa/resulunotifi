The Masked Singer Season 11 returned on Wednesday night for the Semifinals, as the Top 3 contestants performed for their chance to go to the finale. At the end of the show, Goldfish and Gumball made it through, while Clock was unmasked as music legend Thelma Houston.

Clock Unmasked in The Masked Singer Semifinals

During Wednesday’s episode, Clock took the stage to perform “Dancing in the Street.” She later faced off against Gumball and Goldfish in a three-way Battle Royale to Kelly Clarkson’s song “A Moment Like This.” Unfortunately, she didn’t earn enough votes to go to the finale.

Final guesses from the panel about Clock’s identity included Stephanie Mills, Roberta Flack, and Janet Jackson. However, only Rita Ora was correct in guessing that she was Thelma Houston, known for her song “Don’t Leave Me This Way.”

Houston thanked the panel and audience for their kind comments and support throughout the season, telling them that it “made me feel so good.” She closed out her time on the show with an unmasked performance of “Don’t Leave Me This Way.”

Goldfish, Gumball Are in the Finale

Remaining 2 contestants of the season are Goldfish and Gumball. Goldfish and Gumball will be competing in next week’s finale for their chance to earn the coveted Golden Mask trophy and be named the winner of The Masked Singer Season 11.

During Wednesday’s show, Gumball took the stage to perform a heartfelt rendition of “I Lived” by OneRepublic, while Goldfish sang an angsty cover of “You Oughta Know” by Alanis Morissette. Along with Clock, they also opened the show with a performance of “Higher Love.”
