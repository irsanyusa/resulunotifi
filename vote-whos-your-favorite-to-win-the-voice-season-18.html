How To Vote on ‘The Voice’?

There are 3 voting methods for American Idol and you can vote up to 10 times on each of the three methods for your favorite. All together every person can vote 30 times.

When Does ‘The Voice’ Voting Begin and Voting Rules

The voting begins Monday night May 4 at 8pm Eastern time, 5pm Pacific time. Voting opens at the beginning of the show and ends at 7am Eastern time.

You have to be at least 13 years old and located in the U.S., D.C. or Puerto Rico to vote on NBC.com or in the The Voice app.

You can vote up to 10 times per artist, per e-mail regardless of the method. That means if you have gezillion emails, you can technically vote gezillion times 10 and ensure your favorite to make it – good luck with that!

What is ‘The Voice’ Instant Save?

During each of the ‘Results’ episodes some contestants who are in danger of elimination have a chance to be save via the public vote. Unlike previous season’s when The Voice ‘Instant Save’ was through twitter, the voting now takes place on the website or the Voice App. The Instant Save voting window is announced by Carson Daly during the show and votes must be cast during that window of time.
