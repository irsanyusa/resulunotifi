John Cena Explains the Reason Behind His Decision Not to Have Kids and Dedicate 100% of His Love to

August 03, 2024
blog

John Cena, the famous wrestler turned actor, and his wife are happily married without kids. They’ve chosen a child-free lifestyle and are perfectly happy with it. Let’s explore why this loveable hunk has made this decision, and you might find his reasons relatable too.

John Cena didn’t waste much time before tying the knot with his second wife.

Evan Agostini/Invision/AP/East News

John Cena found love with Shay Shariatzadeh, whom he met while working on the movie Playing with Fire in 2019. Their love blossomed quickly, and after just a year and a half of dating, they decided to tie the knot in secret in October 2020.

Just before their joyful date, John Cena tweeted a heartfelt message: “When you are truly ready to let love in and give to love all it needs to flourish, I feel there is no greater more rewarding connection.” It’s clear that he was ready to embrace love and all the happiness it brings. 2 years later, Cena and Shariatzadeh had a proper ceremony to celebrate their love once again.

John Cena has been clear that children are not part of his plan. He and his wife are content with their child-free lifestyle.

Evan Agostini/Invision/AP/East News

When it comes to expanding their family, Cena is taking his time. The Peacemaker star shared that his acting career has taken off, keeping him busy and thoroughly enjoying his current life. He feels incredibly fortunate, having experienced opportunities beyond his wildest dreams.

Cena emphasized that every aspect of his life demands a lot from him, including being his best husband. Balancing everything can be challenging, leaving him with little energy to pursue additional aspirations.

He devotes considerable effort to caring for himself, being a loving partner to his wife, and maintaining connections with those he cares about. It’s evident that he puts his heart and soul into being the best version of himself for the people he loves.

It’s fascinating to note that despite believing he could excel as a parent, the 46-year-old actor doesn’t consider that reason compelling enough to pursue parenthood. According to him, being good at something isn’t sufficient; true passion and genuine drive are essential factors to consider before making such a life-changing decision. For John Cena, the desire to become a parent must come from the heart, filled with passion and genuine enthusiasm.

However, the “kids” conversation is not entirely off the table.

Currently, Cena isn’t ready to start a family with Shariatzadeh. However, reports suggest that having children isn’t entirely off the table for the couple. A close source revealed that John Cena is gradually warming up to the idea and feels more prepared and responsible, especially with Shay.
