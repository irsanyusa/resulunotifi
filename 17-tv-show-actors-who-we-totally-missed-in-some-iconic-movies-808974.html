17 TV Show Actors Who We Totally Missed in Some Iconic Movies

September 03, 2024
blog

Many actors rose to fame thanks to popular TV shows, and viewers tend to associate them with a single character. But if you look closely at the cast of some iconic movies, you may notice some familiar faces among those actors. Jennifer Morrison, for example, who starred in the House TV series, also played an agent in the hit movie, Mr. & Mrs. Smith.

At Bright Side, we studied the filmographies of our favorite actors and found a lot of famous movies in which they appeared, but we simply didn’t notice them.

Jennifer Morrison — House, Mr. & Mrs. Smith

Lauren Graham — Gilmore Girls, Sweet November

Caitriona Balfe — Outlander, Now You See Me

Simon Helberg — The Big Bang Theory, A Cinderella Story

Maggie Wheeler — Friends, The Parent Trap

Leighton Meester — Gossip Girl, The Judge

Andy Samberg — Brooklyn Nine-Nine, What’s Your Number?

Jaime King — Hart of Dixie, Sin City

Lea Michele — Glee, New Year’s Eve

Rebecca Mader — Once Upon a Time, The Devil Wears Prada

Candice King — The Vampire Diaries, Juno

Daniel Gillies — The Originals, Spider-Man 2

Louise Brealey — Sherlock, Victor Frankenstein

Lisa Edelstein — House, What Women Want

Kristin Kreuk — Smallville, EuroTrip

Mischa Barton — The O.C., Notting Hill

Lauren German — Lucifer, A Walk to Remember

Do you know any other TV stars who went unnoticed in some famous movies? Tell us about them in the comments below.
