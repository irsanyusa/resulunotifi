Australian reality star and former Dancing With The Stars celebrity contestant, Harry Jowsey brought his signature brand of lighthearted chaos to actress Jennifer Garner in a new segment for Netflix’s Who Wore It Best? In the episode, Jowsey ambushes Garner wearing a spot-on replica of her eye-catching Versace dress from the 2004 comedy 13 Going On 30.

Harry Jowsey Dresses Like Jennifer Garner From 13 Going On 30

Garner started by introducing herself believing she was being interviewed about the body-swap holiday film. However, a cheeky Jowsey had other plans, abruptly appearing behind a curtain to surprise her.

A stunned Garner then pointed at the dress and then at Jowsey’s butterfly necklace in gleeful disbelief. What followed was a comedic clash of realities with the Aussie pestering the A-lister on her fashion evolution and making unabashed comparisons between Garner’s two body-swapping blockbusters.

In a hilarious moment, Jowsey bluntly asked who wore Jenna Rink’s signature Versace dress best – him or Garner as Jenna from 13 Going on 30?

“Without a doubt you! You’re killing it. Nobody has ever worn this better than you,” Garner said.

Garner even referenced her latest film’s intentional parallels with her breakout 2004 hit, laughing that her co-star was now 13 Going On 30. Clearly, fans can see Family Switch as a homage to Garner’s storied body-swap history.

The actress joins a rapidly growing list of A-listers who have fallen prey to Jowsey’s comedic celebrity ambushes on Who Wore It Best? His chaotic interviews previously included the likes of, Gigi Hadid, Reese Witherspoon, and Ashley Park.

Harry Jowsey Eager For More Reality TV After DWTS

After a successful stint dancing on DWTS Season 32, Jowsey is already eager to return to reality television once again.

“I really love reality television, and this is the third show I’ve been on in the past four months,” Jowsey said. “I just have this big spurt of energy right now where I feel like I need to do television projects.”

The 26-year-old social media star first shot to fame on the Netflix dating show Too Hot To Handle in 2020. He then parlayed that into hosting Netflix fashion series Who Wore It Best as well as competing on The Amazing Race Australia.

While Jowsey’s Mirrorball Trophy dreams may be temporarily dashed, fans likely won’t have to wait long before seeing him bring his signature cheeky persona to his next reality TV venture.
