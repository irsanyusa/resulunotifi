What 14 Celebrities Would Look Like If They Changed Just One Detail of Their Image

June 12, 2024
blog

Have you ever considered changing something about your appearance, like the shape of your nose or the color of your hair? With the help of Photoshop, we decided to use celebrities to reveal how one tiny detail can totally change an overall look as well as remind you that oftentimes, these metamorphoses are not actually necessary.

We at Bright Side like to play with Photoshop because it lets us see many new things without leaving home and helps us come to some valuable conclusions.

Scarlett Johansson

Both of Scarlett Johansson’s lips have the same size and width, which is considered to be ideal. She looks gorgeous with these smaller lips, but even so, nature knows how to create the best look.

Emma Watson

Eye color changes one’s overall appearance drastically and it’s easy to see, even without Photoshop — all you need to do is wear colorful contact lenses. In Emma Watson’s case, we can see, once again, that brown eyes enhance her uniqueness.

Emilia Clarke

Emilia Clarke is one of the most emotional modern actresses and her brows seem to be living a separate life. We tried to change them and realized that she is stunning in both options.

Jake Gyllenhaal

When it came to Jake Gyllenhaal, we realized that even a total change of an overall look doesn’t affect charismatic people like him.

Reese Witherspoon

Reese Witherspoon has a triangle-shaped face with a visibly protruding chin. She has never felt ashamed of it and we’re now positive she’s right.

Jennifer Garner

The actress Jennifer Garner is the owner of big, protruding ears. But she doesn’t feel shy about them and often wears a ponytail or other hairdos that open up her ears. We tried to make them look smaller. The conclusion is: she stayed just as beautiful as she is now.

Eva Mendes

The American actress and model, Eva Mendes, has conquered the hearts of many men. And one of her most outstanding features is the mole on her left cheek. We decided to see what the actress would look like without it and realized that such a small change is not noticeable when a person has such a spectacular appearance and personality.

Dwayne Johnson

“The Rock,” who has more than 200 million followers on Instagram, bewitches with his natural look. After having experimented with his appearance, we realized he’s perfect just the way he is now.

Audrey Tautou

People all over the world recognize Audrey Tautou as the main character from the movie, Amélie. The dimples on her cheeks have warmed all of our souls and it feels as if she’s lacking something without them. Do you agree?

P!nk (Alecia Beth Moore)

Brave and rebellious by nature, P!nk has never tried to show herself as a cute girl. We dared to test this look on her and became convinced that she looks cool the way she is, and there’s nothing that needs to be changed on her.

Uma Thurman

The first thing that many people try to change on themselves is their nose. We tried to make this part of Uma Thurman’s body smaller and we now see that nature makes everything much more harmonious.

Jennifer Lawrence

Many celebrities go to plastic surgeons to make their cheekbones appear sharper. We’re not quite sure who started this trend but we’re convinced that rounded cheeks don’t make anyone look less attractive. If nature has made your face round, it means you look stunning just the way you are.

Margot Robbie

The Australian beauty, Margot Robbie, keeps conquering people’s hearts — not only thanks to her appearance — but also due to her talented acting in various films. Since we’re used to seeing her with fair hair, that’s the very detail we decided to change on her. The conclusion is: a beautiful woman remains beautiful no matter if she has blonde or dark hair.

Gwyneth Paltrow

Gwyneth Paltrow glows from head to toe — just look at her fair hair and brows along with her blue eyes. We changed one detail about her look: her brows. And once again, nature has won over Photoshop with a score of 1 to 0.

Is there something you’d like to change about your appearance?
