20 of the Best Halloween Costumes Celebrities Wore This Year

August 10, 2024
blog

Halloween still can happen without trick-or-treating, pumpkins, or creepy snacks — but not without costumes. And even when it seems like every single costume’s been done before and you know you can’t reinvent the wheel, these celebrities never cease to amaze us with their Halloween getups — like The Weeknd dressed as The Nutty Professor, Cardi B as Medusa, and Janelle Monáe as Chucky. Because no matter how old the idea is, the presentation is all that matters.

Here at Bright Side, we love Halloween and everything that comes along with it. And this time, we couldn’t stay away from all the fuss. So scroll down to see a roundup of celebrities’ best 2020 Halloween costumes.

1. The Weeknd as The Nutty Professor

2. Jennifer Lopez nailed Madonna in her “Like a Virgin” look.

3. Chrissy Teigen baking for her kids in a white swan ballet tutu

4. Janelle Monáe dressed as Chucky

5. Bretman Rock is Edna from The Incredibles.

6. Bella Hadid as a Lichtenstein painting and Poison Ivy

7. Kendall and Kylie Jenner decided to recreate their own childhood Halloween costumes, and they nailed it!

8. “Carole Baskin, Joe Exotic, and our tigers”

9. Halsey as the Corpse Bride

10. Neil Patrick Harris and the family as Charlie and the Chocolate Factory characters

11. Halle Berry dressed as Catrina

12. Christina Aguilera dressed up as an alien.

13. Kylie Jenner as a king cobra

14. Vanessa Hudgens as a black widow

15. Russell Wilson and Ciara dressed as Busta Rhymes and Janet Jackson

16. Bebe Rexha as Poison Ivy

17. Rosalía is an excellent Leeloo from The Fifth Element.

18. Jennifer Garner as an old lady emoji

19. Gabrielle Union-Wade and the family as s’mores!

20. Cardi B as Medusa

What was your costume this year? We’d be happy to see your pictures in the comment section below!
