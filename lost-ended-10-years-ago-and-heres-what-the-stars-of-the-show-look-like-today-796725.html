Lost Ended 10 Years Ago, and Heres What the Stars of the Show Look Like Today

September 12, 2024
blog

It’s been 10 years since the drama Lost was broadcast on ABC. Its enthralling plot twists, cliffhangers, and talented cast made it one of the most talked-about series in the industry. Speaking of the cast members, they have now parted ways and are pursuing their own careers.

Hence, Bright Side caught up on what the Lost passengers look like now and what they are up to.

1. Jorge Garcia as Hugo ’Hurley’ Reyes

2. Evangeline Lilly as Kate Austen

Lilly was found climbing trees in the series many times, this was because of her athletic build, and the fact that she just loved climbing trees. After Lost, she made it big by being a part of the MCU as Wasp, in the Ant-Man movies. She has also worked on The Hobbit movies as Tauriel.

3. Josh Holloway as James ’Sawyer’ Ford

Although. it made him look cool, he personally admitted that he hated his long hair on the show. After Lost, he has been a part of the CBS thriller Intelligence. He also made a cameo appearance in Community and is now starring in a show called Yellowstone as Roarke Carter.

4. Daniel Dae Kim as Jin-Soo Kwon

Daniel Dae Kim reunited with his Lost co-stars Terry O’Quinn and Jorge Garcia in a show called Hawaii Five-0.

5. Emilie de Ravin as Claire Littleton

Ravin is a full-time mom and is also managing her movie career at the same time. In 2019, she starred in a movie called A Lover Scorned as Brooke.

6. Michael Emerson as Ben Linus

Emerson continued with his TV career by starring in a TV series called Evil as Leland Townsend.

7. Yunjin Kim as Sun-Hwa Kwon

Yunjin Kim is now thriving in her career by playing Ms. Ma in a series called Ms. Ma, Nemesis.

8. Terry O’Quinn as John Locke

Since 2019, Terry O’Quinn has been working on a TV Series called Emergence as Richard Kindred.

9. Naveen Richards as Sayid Jarrah

Richards is a British actor who has been a part of Princess Diana’s biopic called Diana and has starred in a show called Instinct as Julian Cousins from 2018 to 2019.

10. Matthew Fox as Dr. Jack Shepherd
