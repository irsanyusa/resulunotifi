Remember contortionist Marina Mazepa from Season 14 of America’s Got Talent? The 22-year-old has some incredible flexibility, and she put it to the test with a crazy dare from photographer Jordan Matter.

Jordan challenged Marina to go about an entire day in contortion. The prize if she could do it? A Lamborghini! Did Marina pull it off? Check out the full challenge in the video below, and prepare to have your mind blown.

Marina Mazepa’s 24-Hour Contortion Challenge

Marina posted the full challenge video on her YouTube account last week. It was filmed during her day off from filming AGT last year, before she was eliminated in the quarterfinals.

The contortionist begins her day sleeping in an impossible position between two walls in her house — complete with a pillow and a sleep mask. She then moves on to brushing her teeth using her feet instead of her hands.

Later, Marina heads to the nail salon to get a pedicure in the most mind-boggling position you can imagine. She also goes shopping, pumps gas, runs on the treadmill, and has lunch with her boyfriend — all in contortion.

Did Marina Win The Lamborghini?

Marina and Jordan ended the challenge by packing Marina into a cardboard box to send her to family in Ukraine. Don’t worry, they didn’t actually mail her anywhere — although she definitely fit in the box.

At the end of the day, Jordan presented Marina with a yellow Lamborghini, and they had a photo shoot. Marina even climbed into the driver’s seat from the roof of the car. She probably shouldn’t try driving the car in any strange positions, though.

Jordan joked that the video would have to get 100 million views for him to pay for the car. The original video on his channel has 4 million views so far, so he has quite a ways to go.
