How to log out of Hulu on your mobile device, smart TV, or computer

August 24, 2024
blog

As a general rule, you don't need to log out of your Hulu account after watching a TV show or movie.

You can stay logged in so you can avoid the login process next time.

But if you're using a shared device or if you logged into a public computer or media player, you'll want to log out when you're done.
