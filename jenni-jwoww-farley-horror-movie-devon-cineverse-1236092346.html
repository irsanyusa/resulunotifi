From Jersey Shore to the Big Screen:Jenni JWoww Farleys Directorial Debut

May 31, 2024
blog

Cineverse has acquired reality star Jenni “JWoww” Farley’s directorial debut “Devon,” which will premiere exclusively on the company’s streaming service Screambox.

The horror film follows a group of friends that tries to learn the truth about an unsolved disappearance but end up at the center of a terrifying mystery. Per the official logline: “Following their daughter’s mysterious disappearance, Devon’s parents never stopped searching for the truth. After the asylum she disappeared from was condemned, their questions looked like they would remain forever unanswered. Years later, a cryptic website recruits five courageous individuals to explore the abandoned asylum and discover what happened to the girl. Unaware that they are embarking on a perilous, one-way journey, these individuals delve into the sinister depths with only their equipment, leading to a terrifying and deadly experience.”

“I am thrilled to be diving into the horror space alongside Cineverse and Screambox who have the industry and fandom knowledge to see that ‘Devon’ lands in the hands of the right audience.” Farley said in a statement. “As a longtime reality TV veteran, I’m excited to kick off this new chapter in the horror movie genre and hope that ‘Devon’ is just the beginning.”

Popular on Variety

Farley is best known for her appearance on the MTV series “Jersey Shore” and its spinoff “Jersey Shore: Family Vacation.” The next season of “Family Vacation” will premiere later this year.

The North American rights for “Devon” were negotiated by Brandon Hill, executive director of acquisitions, and Farley.

“Devon” is set to debut on Screambox on Nov. 12.
