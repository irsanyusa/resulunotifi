Jennifer Aniston and Courteney Cox Honored Lisa Kudrows 60th Birthday in the Sweetest Way Possibl

August 03, 2024
blog

Since the show Friends came to an end in 2004, its female stars Jennifer Aniston, Courteney Cox and Lisa Kudrow have remained very close. Despite their different responsibilities, they always find a way to meet and catch up. On the 30th of July, it was Lisa Kudrow’s 60th birthday and both of her friends made sure to send her their best wishes. Their sweet words prove that friendship never dies out no matter how many years go by.

Lisa Kudrow has had a very fulfilling career so far.

Everyone knows the actress as Phoebe Buffay, but even after Friends ended, she has had many other noteworthy roles. Her mockumentary show The Comeback received very good reviews when it was released back in 2005. Later on, she went on to produce and star in the series Web Therapy that was nominated for an Emmy Award. Apart from her individual projects, she has participated in a plethora of films and series, with some of her most successful films having grossed a total of over $2 billion worldwide.

Jennifer Aniston was the first to wish her happy birthday.

The acclaimed actress made a post on Instagram writing “Please join me in celebrating one of my favorite people on the planet. LISA KUDROW!! She’s been my friend and my family for nearly 30 incredible years. I cherish you...I love you, my sweet Floosh. One of the most talented comedians/actors I’ve had the great honor to work with for all these glorious years and more to come!
Happy Lisa’s Birthday!”

Courteney Cox also chose Instagram to publicly acknowledge her friend.

Sometimes words can’t describe how much love someone has for their friend, but Courtney tried to do so. “Happy Birthday my Loot. This is my second attempt: ChatGPT didn’t give you nearly the amount of love I feel for you. You are the smartest, funniest, most thoughtful person. I always feel seen and loved when I’m around you. That’s the gift you give to those you love.”

The three of them have an unbreakable bond.

With many A-list stars, we often see friendships that fade in the course of time, but that is not the case with these three ladies. They are always there for one another, and they even celebrated Courteney Cox’s star on the Hollywood Walk of Fame in February 2023. They prove that not all famous relationships are fake or manufactured, but they can flourish no matter how many years go by.

Jennifer Aniston and Courteney Cox are known for their tight friendship. Aniston has also revealed how she managed to keep her form and Lisa Kudrow has admitted that she used to be very insecure about her body.
