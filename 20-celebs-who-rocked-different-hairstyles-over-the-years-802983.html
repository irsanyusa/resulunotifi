20 Celebs Whove Rocked Different Hairstyles Over the Years

May 17, 2024
blog

Reese Witherspoon, Jennifer Lawrence, and Cameron Diaz with dark hair, Anne Hathaway with a pixie cut and platinum blonde hair, or Jason Momoa with short hair instead of his gorgeous mane — we’ve gotten used to seeing all these different hairstyles. This may sound unusual, but these are physical changes that actually happened to our favorite celebrities. When famous people take the plunge and suddenly change their signature hairstyles they’ve been rocking for a long time, these dramatic changes can sometimes make our jaws drop.

We at Bright Side recollected 20 times our favorite celebrities changed their signature hairstyles that we all were used to, and we made collages to show those striking transformations. On the left, we showed celebrities with their regular hairstyles, and on the right, their crazy hair experiments.

Cara Delevingne: from platinum blonde with a pixie cut in 2017 to a long and wavy dark blonde in 2022

Andie MacDowell: embracing her natural gray hair

Reese Witherspoon: dazzling blonde instead of a deep dark color

Billie Eilish: blond instead of black and neon green

Michelle Williams: a bob instead of a pixie cut

Leighton Meester: blond instead of dark brown

Jennifer Lawrence: platinum blonde instead of saturated dark hair color

Blake Lively: blonde instead of red hair

Emma Stone: dark hair instead of red hair

Jason Momoa: long hair instead of short hair

Cameron Diaz: platinum blonde instead of dark hair

Kate Hudson: a pixie cut and dark hair instead of her usual long blond hair

Paris Hilton: platinum blonde instead of dark hair

Lindsay Lohan: blonde instead of dark hair

Kendall Jenner: dark hair instead of blonde

Lucy Liu: short hair instead of her usual long hair

Shakira: bright pink instead of blond

Anne Hathaway: from dark hair with a soft curl in 1999 to a blonde pixie cut in 2013, then a fringe with long brown hair in 2022

Mila Kunis: fringe instead of her usual hair cut

Henry Cavill: from a buzzcut in 2008 to a curly hairstyle in 2012, then rocking a classic formal cut in 2019

Which of these celebrities changed their look the most thanks to hair transformations in your opinion? What is the most daring hair experiment you’ve ever done?
