Gina Miles Teases Original Song &quot;Someone New&quot; Since Her Victory on 'The Voice'

The Voice season 23 winner Gina Miles has recently teased her newest song in the works “Someone New.” The promising young star posted a snippet of her raw recording on Instagram, thrilling fans for a possible new song release.

Gina Miles is Working on Another Original Song “Someone New”

Last July 6, Miles announced that she’s currently in the process of creating her newest song. The Voice winner has temporarily titled her brand-new single “Someone New.”

“Wanted to share a little original song i’ve been working on, this one’s Someone New (for now at least lol),” she wrote.

In the clip, Miles is strumming her acoustic guitar as she’s singing a sensational ballad. The musician is using her usual deep and angelic tone to complement her emotional lyrics.

As of writing, Miles hasn’t released any additional details regarding “Someone New.” Her fans, nonetheless, are already excited for the full version of this upcoming brand-new single. Should Miles release this musical piece, such will serve as her first ever single since her win on The Voice.

She Sang the National Anthem for Sacramento Firefighters’ Graduating Class

Last month, the season 23 winner graced Sacramento’s Crest Theater to sing the national anthem at the graduation ceremony of Sacramento Firefighters. According to Miles, being part of such event has been an “important and exciting” experience.

During her interview with KCRA 3, the musician stated that she’s happy to be home and to see her fellow residents celebrate their community.

“Thank you @sacramentofire for having me sing the national anthem for this years fire academy graduation ceremony. what a special experience and congratulations again to all of the recruits,” she wrote on Instagram.

In the post, Miles shares a slideshow of her snaps from the firehouse. The last picture showed The Voice winner holding a beautiful bouquet while smiling for the camera.

With Miles’ recent singing stints, it’s obvious that the young star is still actively pursuing her music career. Indeed, we hope to see her successful in the years to come.
